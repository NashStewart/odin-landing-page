Interesting Axolotl Facts
They can regenerate not just their limbs, but also their jaws, spinal cords, and parts of their brains without scarring.
The axolotl is native to the lake complex of Xochimilco near Mexico City. They are consider extremely endangered.
Wild axolotls are typically brown or black with greenish mottling. Captive axolotls can be various colors.
Their feathery external gills, which look like pompoms, are used to absorb oxygen directly from the water.
